# re-outreach-scripts

Real estate investor outreach scripts built and refined by Lyn (Lynxify).
Covers Facebook Group deal sourcing, Realtor partnerships, motivated seller
outreach, and co-investor lead generation.

These aren't copy-paste blasts. Every script here is built around one rule:
**lead with empathy, close with clarity.**

---

## Who this is for

- Wholesalers sourcing off-market deals via social media
- Fix-and-flip investors building Realtor referral pipelines
- Real estate entrepreneurs looking for passive capital partners
- Marketing specialists managing outreach for RE clients

---

| Folder | What's inside |
|---|---|

## How to use these

1. Pick the script folder that matches your goal
2. Read the **context note** at the top of each file
3. Personalize the `[brackets]` — never send a template cold
4. Test 2–3 variations and track your reply rate
5. If it's not working in 50 sends, the targeting is wrong — not the script

# Facebook Group — Comment Scripts

> **Context:** These are public comments left on posts inside RE investor
> Facebook Groups. The goal is visibility, not a hard sell. You want the
> poster and other members to notice you — and either reply, DM you, or
> visit your profile. Keep it short, specific, and human.

---

## Script 1 — Responding to a "looking to sell" post

[Seller's name], this sounds like it could be a fit for what we're
working on in [city/area]. Are you open to a quick conversation to
see if the numbers make sense for both sides?

No pressure — just want to make sure you're talking to someone who
can actually close.

---

## Script 2 — Responding to a deal posted by another investor

Love seeing deals like this in [area]. Quick question — is this
still available, or do you have a JV / co-wholesaler structure
in place already?

---

## Script 3 — Adding value without asking for anything

Seen a lot of [area] inventory moving this month. If anyone here
is sitting on a property that's been hard to move — distressed,
probate, behind on taxes — feel free to DM me. We close fast and
don't waste your time with lowball games.

---

## Script 4 — Engaging a "looking to buy" post

[Name], what's your buy box in [area]? ARV range, condition
tolerance, min margin? I source off-market and want to make sure
I'm only sending what actually fits.

---

## Tips
- Comment within the first 30 minutes of a post going live
- Never post your phone number in a comment — move to DM first
- Aim for 3–5 meaningful comments per group per day, not spam volume
- # Facebook Group — DM Scripts

> **Context:** Always reference where you saw them or what triggered
> the DM — cold DMs with no context get ignored. Personalize the
> [brackets] before every send. Written for mobile reading: short
> paragraphs, no walls of text.

---

## DM 1 — Following up on a "have a property" post

Hey [Name] — saw your post in [Group Name] about the property in [area].

I work with investors who buy off-market in that zip. Before I pass it
along, can you give me the basics?

- Address (or general area)
- Condition / situation
- What you're looking for price-wise

No wholesaler chains — we deal direct or with one layer max.

— Lyn

---

## DM 2 — Cold reach to someone who commented on a deal

Hey [Name] — noticed you commented on [poster's name]'s deal in [Group].
Looks like you're active in [area].

I source off-market properties there regularly and I'm always looking for
serious buyers who move fast. What's your buy box?

Happy to keep you in the loop if something fits.

— Lyn

---

## DM 3 — Reaching out to a group admin or top contributor

Hey [Name] — I've been active in [Group Name] for a while now and I
always see you keeping things real in there. Respect that.

I specialize in off-market deal sourcing and motivated seller outreach
in [area]. If you ever want to swap leads or explore a referral setup,
I'm open to that conversation.

Either way — keep doing what you're doing.

— Lyn
# Follow-Up Sequence — 3-Touch Series

> **Context:** Most deals don't close on first contact. This sequence
> covers the 3 touches after your opening DM. Stay light — you're
> following up, not chasing.

---

## Touch 1 — Day 3 bump

Hey [Name] — just wanted to make sure this didn't get buried.
Still open to connecting about [property / deal / area]?

---

## Touch 2 — Day 7 value add

[Name] — no worries if the timing's off. For what it's worth,
[area] has been moving fast this month — if anything changes
on your end, I'm easy to find.

---

## Touch 3 — Day 14 soft close

Hey [Name] — I'll stop nudging after this. If the situation with
[property / deal] changes down the road, feel free to circle back.
I keep a short list of people I'd want to work with — you're on it.

— Lyn

# Realtor Outreach — Cold Scripts

> **Context:** Realtors get pitched constantly. The ones who respond
> are the ones who feel like you actually understand their world.
> Lead with a specific insight about their market, not a generic ask.

---

## Script 1 — Instagram/Facebook DM

Hey [Agent Name] — saw your listing on [street/area]. Looks like
you've got a solid read on [neighborhood].

I work with cash buyers actively looking in that pocket. Not here
to step on your toes — just want to know if you'd ever be open to
a referral relationship when you come across a seller who needs to
move fast and doesn't want to list.

Worth a 10-minute call?

— Lyn, Lynxify

---

## Script 2 — Email outreach

Subject: Quick question about your [area] listings

Hi [Agent Name],

I came across your profile while researching active agents in [area].
Your recent sales in [neighborhood] caught my attention — you clearly
know that pocket well.

I'm a real estate marketing and outreach specialist who works with cash
buyers and investors looking for off-market or pre-market opportunities
in your area. I'm not trying to cut you out of a deal — quite the
opposite. I want to refer motivated sellers your way when the situation
calls for a traditional listing, and in return, I'd love to be your
go-to when a seller needs a fast cash close.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week?

— Lyn

# Motivated Seller — DM Scripts

> **Context:** Distressed sellers are not leads — they're people in
> hard situations. Lead with empathy, ask questions, and let them feel
> heard before you ever mention numbers.

---

## DM 1 — Responding to a "need to sell fast" post

Hey [Name] — I saw your post. I'm not going to blow up your inbox
with a lowball offer like everyone else probably has.

I just want to ask: what does your ideal outcome look like?
Fast close? Flexibility on move-out? Something else?

Understanding that first helps me figure out if I'm even the right
fit for you.

— Lyn

---

## DM 2 — Probate / tax situation

Hi [Name] — I came across your post in [group]. Situations like
yours are something I navigate regularly, and I know how much
pressure that puts on a family.

I work with investors who handle everything — liens, back taxes,
condition issues — so you don't have to fix anything or wait for
a bank to approve a buyer.

Would it help to just talk through what you're dealing with?
No commitment, no pressure.

— Lyn

# Content Hooks — Organic Posts That Attract Sellers

> **Context:** These are social media post templates designed to
> surface motivated sellers organically — no cold outreach needed.
> Post consistently and let inbound do the work.

---

## Hook 1 — The permission post

If you're sitting on a property you've been meaning to deal with
for months — and you don't know where to start — this is your sign
to just ask. No judgment. No pressure. DM me.

---

## Hook 2 — The reframe

You don't have to fix it up.
You don't have to stage it.
You don't have to wait for a buyer to get financing.

Some sellers just want it done. We do that.

---

## Hook 3 — The pain point

Inherited a property you don't want to manage?
Behind on taxes and not sure what your options are?
Divorce and need to split an asset fast?

These aren't problems. They're situations — and they have solutions.

---

## Hook 4 — The social proof

Closed with a seller last [month] who'd been trying to figure out
what to do with their late parent's home for 14 months.
We closed in 11 days.

Sometimes people just need someone to make it simple.
# Co-Investor Outreach — Intro Scripts

> **Context:** These are for reaching passive investors or JV partners
> — people with capital who want deal flow without sourcing themselves.
> Lead with the opportunity, not the pitch.

---

## Script 1 — Cold intro DM

Hey [Name] — I source off-market deals in [area/region] and I'm
building a short list of capital partners to loop in when the
numbers are right.

Not looking for a commitment now — just want to know if off-market
[fix-and-flip / DSCR / buy-and-hold] in [area] is something you'd
want to hear about when the right one comes up.

What's your preferred deal structure?

— Lyn

---

## Script 2 — Sharing a deal

Hey [Name] — got something that fits what you mentioned.

Property: [area]
ARV: ~$[X]
Estimated repair: $[X]
Asking: $[X]
Deal type: [wholesale / JV / co-wholesale]

Happy to send the full dossier if you want to take a look.
Can move on this by [date] — let me know.

— Lyn

---

## Script 3 — Post-call follow-up

[Name] — great talking earlier. Sending over the deal summary
we discussed.

A few things to note:
- Title is clean
- Seller is motivated, no agent involved
- Comps support the ARV I mentioned

Let me know by [date] if you want to move forward. If it's not
the right fit, no problem — I'll keep you in the loop on the next one.

— Lyn
